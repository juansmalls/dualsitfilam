Here I've created a basic list on the process of Reporting your birth Abroad to the Philippine Consulate via mail. Please keep in mind that situations vary depending on the persons circumstance. You may need extra docucments/affidavits explaining why you cannot send in certain documents. Example:If parents were not married at the time of you birth you may need to fill out a notarized affidavit proving the identity of both parents.

  1. Cashiers Check

  2. Report Of Birth Cover Letter

  3. Report of Birth Form

  4. Certificate of Live Birth/Long form Birth Certificate

  5. Marriage Certificate of Parents

  6. Copy of documents proving parents past or present Philippine citizenship (passport, Greencard, Birth Certificate, US Naturalization Certificate)

  7. Copy of fathers Passport or Valid drivers liscense
